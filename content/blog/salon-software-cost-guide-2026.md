Choosing salon software is a major decision. Here's everything you need to know about pricing in 2026.

## The True Cost of Salon Software

Most salon software has multiple cost components:

### 1. Monthly Subscription
- **Budget tier:** £15-£30/month
- **Mid-tier:** £50-£150/month
- **Enterprise:** £200-£500+/month

### 2. Setup Fees
- **Common range:** £0-£1,000
- **Enterprise:** £2,000-£10,000+

### 3. Transaction Fees
- **Per-transaction:** 1-10% of sale
- **Monthly cap:** Sometimes available
- **Payment processing:** Additional 2-3%

### 4. Hidden Costs
- Training (£500-£2,000)
- Data migration (£300-£1,500)
- Hardware (£500-£3,000)
- SMS credits (£50-£200/month)
- Additional users (£10-£50/user/month)

## Pricing Models Explained

### Flat Monthly Fee (Best Value)

**Example:** BookB at £15/month

**Pros:**
- Predictable costs
- No surprise fees
- Scales with your business

**Best for:** Independent salons, small businesses

### Percentage-Based Pricing

**Example:** Fresha at 0-10% per transaction

**Pros:**
- Lower upfront cost
- Pay as you grow

**Cons:**
- Costs can spiral
- Discourages sales
- Hard to predict

**Best for:** Very small salons testing software

### Tiered Pricing

**Example:** Most salon software

**Pros:**
- Choose features you need
- Upgrade as you grow

**Cons:**
- Easy to outgrow tiers
- Features locked behind paywalls

## Common Pricing Traps

### 1. "Free" Software
Usually means:
- High transaction fees (5-10%)
- Limited features
- Aggressive upselling
- Hidden costs

### 2. Long-Term Contracts
Watch out for:
- 12-24 month commitments
- Auto-renewal clauses
- Cancellation penalties
- Price increases

### 3. Per-User Pricing
Can get expensive with:
- Multiple stylists
- Part-time staff
- Seasonal workers

### 4. Feature Restrictions
Cheaper tiers often lack:
- Queue management
- Advanced reporting
- API access
- Priority support

## How to Save Money

### 1. Choose the Right Tier
Don't pay for features you don't need, but don't choose too small either.

### 2. Annual Payment Discounts
Most providers offer 15-20% off for annual payment.

### 3. Avoid Transaction Fees
Software with flat pricing saves money long-term.

### 4. Bundle Services
Some providers offer:
- Bundled payment processing
- Included SMS credits
- Free training
